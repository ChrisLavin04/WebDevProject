Services

At Lavin's Pet Essentials, we go above and beyond to cater to all your pet's needs. Explore our wide range of services designed to keep your furry friends happy, healthy, and well-cared for.

Types of Services

  • Grooming: Treat your pet to a pampering session at our grooming salon! Our experienced groomers offer a variety of services, including baths, haircuts, nail trims, and ear cleaning. Choose from our selection of grooming packages to find the perfect option for your pet's needs.
  • Pet Boarding and Daycare: Going out of town? Leave your pet in good hands at our boarding facility or daycare center. Our spacious accommodations and dedicated staff ensure that your pet receives the love, attention, and exercise they need while you're away.
  • Pet Sitting and Walking: Need someone to care for your pet while you're at work or on vacation? Our pet sitting and walking services have got you covered. Our professional pet sitters and walkers will ensure that your pet receives the care and attention they deserve, whether it's a quick walk around the block or an overnight stay.
